# Distill-and-Publish

## Also Known As
Knowledge Embodiment

## Problem
Vault knowledge and creative outputs are trapped inside the system — no external audience can benefit from them, and the system receives no feedback from the world.

## Context
Any knowledge system that produces original insights but has no channel for publication or feedback.

## Forces
Private knowledge is safe but has no impact. Publication requires curation, quality thresholds, and confidence. Feedback is the fastest training signal but can be negative.

## Solution
Automatically distill top-ranking insights into structured publications (essays, threads, whitepapers). Score by novelty, value alignment, and confidence. Require human approval before any external publication. Capture feedback signals back into the knowledge base.

## Resulting Context
Knowledge gains real-world traction. The system learns what resonates. Requires human review bottleneck, which limits throughput but maintains quality.
